About ISM

Independent Study and Mentorship

Frisco ISD's Independent Study and Mentorship program is a program where students must apply, interview, and be recommended for in their high school junior or senior year. Students who enter the program then focus on a topic of their choice, developing a research portfolio consisting of resources like peer-reviewed articles and interviews with professionals in the field.

Students develop time management skills, soft skills, presentation skills, and networking skills as they work towards an original work for the fall semester and a final product for the spring semester. In the spring semester, students select a mentor working in their field of choice to gain real-world experience and develop their final product.

Students conclude the program with a final presentation of their product and gain knowledge about their chosen profession as they prepare to enter college.
